# GAME DESIGN

## Light Academy OS

The purpose of game design is not entertainment.

The purpose is to motivate children to train every day.

---

# Core Principle

Learning should feel like progress.

Never punishment.

Always encouragement.

---

# Daily Training

Training duration:

5–10 minutes.

Children should finish with the feeling:

"I want to do one more."

---

# Daily Streak

Every completed day increases the streak.

Examples:

1 day

5 days

10 days

30 days

100 days

If one day is missed:

The streak pauses.

No punishment.

---

# Experience Points (XP)

Every exercise gives XP.

XP unlocks:

- new levels
- achievements
- rewards

# Achievements

Examples:

First Training

10 Days

100 Correct Answers

Perfect Accuracy

Fast Thinker

Memory Master

Mental Calculator

---

# Daily Challenge

Every day has one challenge.

Example:

Complete one training.

Reward:

Extra XP.

---

# Weekly Challenge

Train five days this week.

Reward:

Special badge.

---

# Progress

Children should always see:

- today's result
- best score
- improvement
- next goal

Never compare children publicly.

Compare only with their own previous performance.

---

# Rewards

Rewards are symbolic.

Examples:

Badges

Stars

Owls

New themes

Animations

Certificates

No gambling mechanics.

No random rewards.

---

# Feedback

Positive.

Examples:

Great job!

You were faster today!

Excellent focus!

New personal record!

---

# Difficulty

The system should adapt gradually.

Not too easy.

Not too difficult.

The goal is Flow.

---

# Design Rules

Simple.

Fast.

Bright.

Calm.

No advertisements.

No unnecessary clicks.

One main action per screen.

---

# Ultimate Goal

Children return every day because they enjoy improving themselves.

Learning becomes a habit.
