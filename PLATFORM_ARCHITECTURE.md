# Light Academy OS

## Vision

Light Academy OS is a complete digital ecosystem for lifelong learning.

One platform.

One account.

Unlimited learning.

The platform serves:

- Children
- Parents
- Teachers
- Mentors
- Partners
- Schools

- Analytics
- AI Services
- Settings
